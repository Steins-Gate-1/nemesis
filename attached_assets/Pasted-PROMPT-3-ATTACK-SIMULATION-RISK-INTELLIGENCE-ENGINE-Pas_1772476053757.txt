📊 RISK CALCULATION FRAMEWORK

Define deterministic scoring.

Likelihood Score based on:

• Exposure severity
• Number of vulnerabilities
• Availability of credentials
• Ease of exploitation

Impact Score based on:

• Critical infrastructure exposure
• Credential privilege level
• Number of open services
• Type of data exposed

Risk Score =

likelihood × impact

Map result to:

LOW
MODERATE
HIGH
CRITICAL

No randomness allowed.
