🛡️ PROMPT: Build the Deepfake Warfare Defense Unit for NEMESIS

Act as a senior AI security researcher and digital forensics engineer designing the Deepfake Warfare Defense Unit for a national-level cyber defense system called NEMESIS.

This module must protect organizations and high-value individuals from AI-based impersonation attacks including synthetic video, synthetic voice, and manipulated images.

No theoretical discussion.
No vague AI detection claims.
Only implementable, API-driven, production-ready design.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Design a Deepfake Defense module that:

• Calculates Deepfake Exposure Risk
• Detects synthetic media (video, audio, image)
• Produces confidence scores
• Logs detection events
• Integrates with risk engine
• Generates mitigation guidance

The system must use hosted detection APIs or deployable detection services — not local heavy ML training.

🧠 MODULE 1 — DEEPFAKE EXPOSURE SCORING

Purpose:

Assess how vulnerable a person or organization is to impersonation.

INPUT

• Public video links
• Public interviews
• Public speaking duration
• Public image presence

EXPOSURE METRICS

Calculate:

1️⃣ Total public video duration
2️⃣ Audio clarity rating
3️⃣ Face visibility frequency
4️⃣ Image resolution availability
5️⃣ Number of publicly accessible interviews

EXPOSURE SCORE FORMULA

Example deterministic model:

Exposure Score =

(video_minutes × 0.4) +
(audio_quality × 0.2) +
(face_visibility × 0.2) +
(image_availability × 0.2)

Normalize to 0–100.

Classify:

0–30 → LOW
31–60 → MODERATE
61–80 → HIGH
81–100 → CRITICAL

DATABASE TABLE

deepfake_exposure_profiles:

• id
• subject_name
• video_minutes
• audio_score
• face_visibility_score
• exposure_score
• exposure_level
• created_at
